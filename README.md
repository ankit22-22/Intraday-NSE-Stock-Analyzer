# Intraday NSE Stock Analyzer

## Project Overview

The Intraday NSE Stock Analyzer is a financial analytics dashboard
designed to monitor and analyze intraday price movements of Nifty50 and
70+ top-weighted NSE constituent stocks across multiple sectors. The
system supports data-driven stock selection by tracking sector
performance, price momentum, trading volume, and comparative market
trends.

The project follows industry-standard financial analytics practices,
focusing on structured data analysis, sector-based segmentation, and
decision-support visualization.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Business Objective

Intraday trading requires rapid decision-making based on real-time
market signals. This project aims to:

-   Identify high-momentum stocks within Nifty50
-   Compare sector-wise performance
-   Track trading volume and price volatility
-   Support systematic stock filtering for intraday strategies

The dashboard acts as a decision-support system rather than a prediction
engine.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Dashboard Components Explained

### 1. Sector Performance Overview

Displays sector-wise performance metrics to identify leading and lagging
industries during the trading session.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### 2. Intraday Price Movement Analysis

Tracks percentage price change of individual stocks to identify
high-momentum opportunities.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### 3. Trading Volume Monitoring

Monitors volume spikes and volume-price relationships to confirm trade
strength.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### 4. Sector Weightage Impact

Shows how sector and stock weightage influence overall Nifty movement.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### 5. Comparative Performance Panel

Benchmarks stock performance against sector and Nifty index for relative
strength analysis.
